Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

Cedratech Consulting Services Ltd. (“Cedratech,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your personal data and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) (“GDPR”) and other applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data when you visit our website at https://cedratech.com or otherwise interact with us, and describes the rights available to you under the GDPR.

Data Controller

For the purposes of the GDPR, Cedratech Consulting Services Ltd. is the data controller responsible for personal data collected through this website. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you may contact us at info@cedratech.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data: contact and identification data, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company name, provided when you complete a contact or inquiry form; technical data, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data collected automatically via cookies and similar technologies; and communications data, being the content of any messages, attachments, or other information you send us.

Legal Basis for Processing

We only process personal data where we have a valid legal basis under Article 6 of the GDPR, including: your consent, where you have given clear consent for a specific purpose; performance of a contract, where processing is necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract, or to perform a contract with you; legitimate interests, where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as responding to inquiries or maintaining website security, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights; and legal obligation, where processing is necessary for us to comply with the law.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal data to respond to your inquiries and provide the services or information you request, to operate, maintain, and improve our website and services, to communicate with you regarding our business relationship, and to comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations. We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how visitors use our site. Where required by law, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies, and you can manage or withdraw that consent at any time through your browser settings or any cookie preference tool provided on this site.

Contact Forms and Your Right to Deletion

When you contact us, for example through a contact or inquiry form or by email, we collect and process the personal data you provide solely to respond to your request and, where relevant, to follow up regarding our services. You have the right to request that we delete any personal data we hold about you that was collected through your contact with us. To make such a request, please email info@cedratech.com, and we will act on verified requests without undue delay and in any event within one month, as required by the GDPR, unless we are legally required or permitted to retain certain data.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share personal data with trusted third-party service providers who process data on our behalf, such as website hosting, email, or analytics providers, strictly for the purposes described in this policy and under appropriate data processing agreements. We may also disclose personal data where required to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or governmental request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

International Data Transfers

If personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses, an adequacy decision, or another mechanism recognized under the GDPR, to protect your data.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including to satisfy any applicable legal, accounting, or reporting requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights Under the GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data, subject to applicable legal limitations: the right to access the personal data we hold about you; the right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data; the right to erasure of your personal data; the right to restrict or object to our processing of your data; the right to data portability; the right to withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal; and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country of residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement, if you believe our processing of your personal data violates the GDPR. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@cedratech.com.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Where we are required to notify a personal data breach under the GDPR, we will do so in accordance with applicable legal timeframes and requirements.

Limitation of Liability

This Privacy Policy describes our data practices in good faith and to the best of our knowledge. To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Cedratech Consulting Services Ltd. shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the processing of personal data, except where such liability cannot be excluded under the GDPR or other mandatory law. Nothing in this policy limits any statutory rights you may have as a data subject.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our data practices, technology, or applicable law. Material changes will be posted on this page along with a revised effective date, and, where required, we will notify you through appropriate means.

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR where applicable, without prejudice to any mandatory consumer protection or data protection rights available to you under the laws of your country of residence.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our handling of your personal data, including requests to access, correct, or delete your data, please contact us at info@cedratech.com.
