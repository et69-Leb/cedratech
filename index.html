Digital Finance · Digital Payments · Digital Public Infrastructure

The advisory partner for the institutions building the future of digital finance.

Cedratech is a boutique consultancy working where financial institutions, regulation, and technology meet — designing the payment systems, digital public infrastructure, and institutional capabilities that resilient, inclusive economies depend on.

A specialist firm for a specialized market.

We work exclusively in digital financial services — a niche that rewards depth over breadth. From national payment rails and digital public infrastructure to payment gateways, terminal fleets, agency banking, and inclusive lending, our engagements share one thread: helping institutions move from strategy to systems that work, and run them with confidence long after we leave.
